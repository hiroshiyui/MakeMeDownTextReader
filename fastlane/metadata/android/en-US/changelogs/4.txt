First F-Droid release.

* Native reader and editor for EPUB, Markdown, and plain text.
* No WebView, no network, no analytics.
* Compose editor with a new-file flow.
* Reader bottom bar: zoom, font-size, user-selectable font.
* Solarized theme, Roboto Slab interface font.
* Biometric-locked private documents space, excluded from backup
  and device-to-device transfer.
* Every share from the private space needs a fresh biometric check.
* Bundled samples: UDHR (français), Little Women (EPUB).
